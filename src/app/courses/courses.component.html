Course Overview

This course explores the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and sports analytics, focusing on football (soccer), basketball, and ice hockey. Participants will learn how to leverage cutting-edge AI models to analyze player performance, optimize strategies, and predict outcomes. The course combines theoretical foundations with hands-on applications, using real-world datasets and state-of-the-art techniques.

Course Structure

Module 1: Introduction to AI in Sports Analytics

  • Subtitles: The Rise of Data in Sports, Overview of AI Models, Key Challenges in Sports Data
  • Topics: Role of AI in sports analytics, types of data, spatiotemporal challenges

Module 2: Video Processing for Player and Ball Detection

  • Subtitles: Computer Vision in Sports, Detecting Players and Tracking the Ball
  • Topics: Object detection, pose estimation, multi-object tracking

Module 3: Analyzing Event and Tracking Data with AI Models

  • Subtitles: Advanced Metrics with AI, Explainable AI for Sports
  • Topics: xG, xT, VAEP models, player ranking, case studies

Module 4: Spatiotemporal Models for Tracking Data

  • Subtitles: Spatial and Temporal Analysis, Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)
  • Topics: Player interactions, temporal analysis, passing networks

Module 5: Clustering Possessions and Playing Style Analysis

  • Subtitles: Clustering Possessions with AI, Dimensionality Reduction
  • Topics: Clustering techniques, visualizing playing styles

Module 6: Reinforcement Learning for Action Valuation and Optimization

  • Subtitles: Reinforcement Learning in Sports, Valuing Actions with RL
  • Topics: Q-Learning, Deep RL, optimizing in-game decisions

Module 7: In-Game Outcome Prediction

  • Subtitles: AI for Outcome Forecasting, Bayesian Models
  • Topics: Win probability estimation, real-time predictions

Module 8: Practical Applications and Future Directions

  • Subtitles: Building AI Tools for Coaches, Ethical Considerations
  • Topics: AI-driven dashboards, ethical challenges, emerging technologies
